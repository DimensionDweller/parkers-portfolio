Neural Style Transfer

This project showcases a Neural Style Transfer model built on top of the VGG19 architecture, trained to apply the style of a given image to another while preserving the content of the original image.

Novel Face Generation & Manipulation - Variational auto encoder (VAE) Implimentation

This project presents a Variational Autoencoder (VAE) trained on the CelebA dataset for 50 epochs. The model can generate novel images of faces, demonstrating impressive results. Two additional features are also provided: a function to display the distribution of the latent space variables and another to manipulate novel images by performing arithmetic in the latent space.

An Investigation into the relationship between climate-related financing and the decoupling of c02 emissions & economic growth

Given that the amount of investments into developing countries to
help accelerate their decoupling journey is significant, our team
wanted to investigate these financial flows and try to determine if
these investments have helped countries lower emissions, all while
still maintaining or growing their economy.
